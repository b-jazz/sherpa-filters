Regular Expressions

Regular Expressions (regex's) are kind of difficult to explain. Regex's are very powerful way of describing a range of possible text values.

Say, for instance, that your friend Tom can send you mail from many different computers in the same company. One day you might get mail from tom@pinky.company.com and the next day, you might get mail from tom@brain.company.com. If you want to express both of those addresses with one statement, you could write tom@.*\.company\.com.

There are so many types of regex's that it would be impossible for me to document them all. What you should really do is visit the Microsoft site that documents the complete regular expressions that Sherpa makes use of.
