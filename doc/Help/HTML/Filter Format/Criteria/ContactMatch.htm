ContactMatch

Use the ContactMatch criteria to match an email address in the email to all of the addresses listed in your Contacts.

You can specify one Header field. Valid fields are From, To, Cc, and ToCc. The ToCc header is just a combination of the addresses in the To: and Cc: headers.

The ContactsName is the path to your Contacts database. The default path in Outlook is just Contacts. Contrary to the graphic above, you cannot currently specify more than just the default Contacts. A later version of Sherpa will allow this.

ContactMatch Example

This is a pretty simple example that shows you how to see if email messages are from someone that you have in your Contacts database.

<Criteria>
  <ContactMatch>
    <Header>From</Header>
    <ContactsName>Contacts</ContactsName>
  </ContactMatch>
</Criteria>

This is a more interesting rule since we are seeing if the email is sent to someone you know. This is great in the situation where a friend of yours sends mail to you an a couple of other friends (that you don't know). You'll get his mail with the example above, but if one of his friends does a reply-all, the example below will match since it is to someone (your friend) that is in your Contact.

<Criteria>
  <ContactMatch>
    <Header>ToCc</Header>
    <ContactsName>Contacts</ContactsName>
  </ContactMatch>
</Criteria>
