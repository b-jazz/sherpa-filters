Or

Combines two or more criteria. If any of the sub-criteria match, then the overall criteria triggers a match.

As the graphic above demonstrates, you can next any criteria inside an Or criteria. This even includes more And, Or, and Not critera, so you can have very complex statements.

Or Example

I've been banging my head on this for a while trying to come up with a good example of when to use the Or criteria, but I just can't find one. The usage is simple enough though and looks very similar to the And criteria. Most situations can be handled by the fact that several of the other criteria have implicit Or's built into them.
