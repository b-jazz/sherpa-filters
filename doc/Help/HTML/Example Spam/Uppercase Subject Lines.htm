Uppercase Subject Lines

It isn't as popular a spamming signature as it used to be, but I still see the spam come through with the subject line screaming at you in all caps. Once again, making use of regular expressions can easily take care of this problem. Look for a series of 15 or more uppercase letters, numbers, spaces, and other punctuation (like '!' and '$') and mark it as a spam message.

Uppercase Example

<Filter Name="SPAM: Uppercase Subject Line" Enabled="true" Tag="Spam">
  <Criteria>
    <HeaderMatch>
      <Header>Subject</Header>
      <Value>[A-Z0-9\W]{15,}</Value>
    </HeaderMatch>
  </Criteria>
  <Actions>
    <Move>
      <Destination>Mailboxes/Spam</Destination>
    </Move>
  </Actions>
  <Stats>
    <TestCounter>0</TestCounter>
    <MatchCounter>0</MatchCounter>
    <FilterTime>0</FilterTime>
    <LastMatchTime></LastMatchTime>
  </Stats>
</Filter>
