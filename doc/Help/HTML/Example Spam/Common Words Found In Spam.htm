Common Words Found In Spam

First off, let me warn you that the words you'll find in this page of the documentation are rated 'R'. They are offensive to many, and that is why we want to filter them out.

Many of the spam message that you'll receive have some very common words in them that you just don't find in your normal, day-to-day emails. And if you order your filters so that your legitimate email from friends and coworkers get filtered first, you won't throw away an email message from a colleague that claims, "my last project was raped by management."

Example

<Filter Name="SPAM: Common Words In Spam" Enabled="true" Tag="Spam">
  <Criteria>
    <BodyMatch>
      <Value>penis</Value>
      <Value>adult</Value>
      <Value>rape</Value>
      <Value>unsubscribe</Value>
      <Value>opt-in</Value>
      <Value>special offer</Value>
      <Value>money</Value>
      <Value>free</Value>
      <Value>teens</Value>
      <Value>visit</Value>
    </BodyMatch>
  </Criteria>
  <Actions>
    <Move>
      <Destination>Mailboxes/Spam</Destination>
    </Move>
  </Actions>
  <Stats>
    <TestCounter>0</TestCounter>
    <MatchCounter>0</MatchCounter>
    <FilterTime>0</FilterTime>
    <LastMatchTime></LastMatchTime>
  </Stats>
</Filter>
